# Project Purpose

## Why the Hashboard exists

The Zettahash Hashboard is the public-facing transparency layer for the Zettahash DAO. It exists to make operational performance and governance activity understandable to tokenholders, contributors, and prospective partners in a single, coherent interface.

## Purpose statement

Provide a single source of truth for Zettahash operations by combining mining performance, treasury visibility, market context, and governance participation into an accessible dashboard.

## Target outcomes

- Increase trust by making operational data easy to verify.
- Reduce time-to-insight for tokenholders reviewing performance.
- Encourage governance participation by surfacing Snapshot proposals in context.
- Offer clear next actions (connect wallet, view vesting, participate, or purchase ZH).

## Non-goals

- Replace on-chain explorers or exchange interfaces.
- Act as a full trading terminal.
- Provide custodial wallet features.

## Success criteria (qualitative)

- Users can answer core questions in under 2 minutes:
  - How is mining performance trending?
  - What is current treasury posture?
  - What governance votes are open?
- Data is easy to audit and trace back to sources.
- The UI remains stable and readable on desktop and mobile.
